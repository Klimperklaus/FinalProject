IT'S NO JOURNEY, JUST HATE !

Betrayed by his loved ones, robbed of his job by shady characters and ultimately a broken man... You are Liam Agar, a former model member of British society who was maltreated for no reason by his loved ones and superiors until he could no longer bear the endless torment and took his own life. Once in hell, a demon offers to reduce his torment in return for a sufficient supply of souls. This is where the horror begins, which causes Liam to deteriorate more and more until he meets his former "friends". This is where the true horror begins....
